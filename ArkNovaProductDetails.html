Item Description:

Ark nova is a competitive points based simulation game. Collect animals and zoo features and use them to build out your zoo grid. Build enclosures to increase the number of animals you can keep in your zoo and collect development cards to improve you ability to score in the game.The final scoring of the game is based on presentation points which are logged on a competitive track. Choose from five actions every turn, which gain strength based on their position and frequency of use. A fun game for ages 10 and up.
