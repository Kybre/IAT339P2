Basic Branding Ideas

What does the company do, or offer?

Dawning Corporation is a dark/gothic fantasy-inspired digital storefront that focuses on selling tabletop accessories for Tabletop Roleplaying Games (TTRPGs).

Examples of products include themed dice sets, trays, miniature figurines, stationary, and many more.

Who is the company to potential customers? (as a person)

As a company, Dawning Corporation lives and dies by the unique products they offer. They are the slightly mysterious, but fun friend to have around, with a deep knowledge of how the tabletop community wants to perfect their gaming experiences.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

This company may entice someone to purchase their products as a one-stop shop for Tabletop Roleplaying Games, as opposed to individual merchants who specialize in selling individualized accessories. This would be highly valued to dedicated players and Dungeon Masters who are looking specifically for gothic-themed accessories for their TTRPG tables, with potential applications for household decorum as well.

Customers can expect high-quality products across various price ranges, as well as thematic bundles within the gothic fantasy genre that can make the shopping experience easier.
