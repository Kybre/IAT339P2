About Us

We are The Niche!

A place for Niche games and Coffee. We have a choice selection of games for all ages and players to rent or own. We take our coffee as seriously as our gaming, so you can enjoy a sip or meal while you game with your friends or family.

Who We Are

The Niche was founded as a partnership between co owners Nicholas Byrne and Herman Chan. Nicholas Byrne and Herman Chan are gamers trained in design and tech, and they hope that their background and understanding of the gaming world will alow them to provide a quality experience for all levels of gamer.

A Culture of Games

The business was founded with the goal of creating a gathering place for members of the gaming comunity, as well as a retreat for cassual gamers and people who may not game in the current market. We welcome all level and type of gamer, and facilitate classic board gaming as well as fantasy, social, and childrens gaming.

Join Us!

The Niche is dedicated to providing both a quality cafe experience and an inviting gaming environment. If you are either an avid gamer or a skilled barista, then the Niche has a place for you! You can call us at our company phone number or our email.
