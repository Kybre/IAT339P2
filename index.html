Welcome to The Niche

Your one-stop shop for a Games and Coffee.

A place for friends and family.

The Niche is more than just a coffee shop and more than just a Game Store. We hope to provide the best of both experiences. The Niche was founded in order to create a cosy and inviting experience by providing both fantastic food and drink, and the best selection of board games to rent or own.

Featured Event: Sunday Night Magic

Magic Card tournaments are held at The Niche every Sunday. Bring your deck and join us at 5:00 pm for games and treats. Holding both draft tournaments and EDH tournaments.
